Who We Are

Turbo Company is a professional driving education institution committed to providing structured, safe, and comprehensive training programs. With an emphasis on road safety and modern instructional techniques, we ensure every participant develops the required competence to drive responsibly.

Our History

Founded in 2021 by automotive enthusiasts, Turbo Course grew from a small community into a modern academy known for its practical, energetic, and easy-to-follow teaching style designed for the new generation.

Our Philosophy

  • Safe: Safety is always the first priority.
  • Skilled: Strong, measurable driving skills.
  • Confident: Calmness and readiness behind the wheel.

Our Vision

To become Indonesia’s leading driving training institution that produces safe, skilled, and responsible drivers.

Core Values

Safety First • Professionalism • Trust • Integrity

Our Mission

  • Deliver high-quality and accessible programs.
  • Promote road safety as our foundation.
  • Maintain certified instructors and top-tier vehicles.
  • Integrate technology and digital evaluation.
