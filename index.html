Company Overview

Turbo Company is a professional driving education institution committed to providing structured, safe, and comprehensive training programs for aspiring drivers. With an emphasis on road safety, confidence building, and modern instructional techniques, we ensure that every participant develops the required competence to drive responsibly.

Our History

Founded in 2021 by automotive enthusiasts, Turbo Course saw the need for a driving school that was modern and engaging. What began as a small community quickly grew into a premier academy known for professional young instructors and a commitment to the new generation of drivers.

Our Philosophy

  • Safe: Safety is always the first priority in theory and practice.
  • Skilled: Structured lessons to develop measurable skills.
  • Confident: Building calmness and readiness behind the wheel.

Vision

To become Indonesia’s leading driving training institution that produces safe, skilled, and responsible drivers.


Mission

  • Deliver high-quality and accessible training.
  • Promote road safety as a foundation.
  • Maintain professionalism through certified instructors.
  • Integrate technology and digital evaluation tools.

Core Values

Safety First Professionalism Trust Integrity Continuous Learning
